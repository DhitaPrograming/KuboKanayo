About KuboKanayo Creative

KuboKanayo Creative is a B2C-focused creative agency that builds standout brand campaigns powered by bold 2D and 3D visual storytelling.

We specialize in awareness-driven strategies that help consumer brands rise above the noise and become unforgettable. From motion graphics and digital design to product-focused content and immersive brand visuals, we craft creative that moves people — and moves culture. Whether you’re launching a product or looking to reconnect with your audience, we turn your message into campaigns that resonate, engage, and drive lasting attention.

Our Philosophy: Kubo & Kanayo

Kubo is inspired by the warmth of a humble space, a place to think, dream, and build. In Indonesian and Filipino culture, kubo refers to a small hut or shelter, simple, grounded, yet full of life and stories.

Kanayo is a name we gave meaning to, a symbol of belief, ambition, and identity. It represents the dreamer who dares, the creator who disrupts, and the soul who leads with culture.

Together, "KuboKanayo" is more than a name — it’s a mindset. A fusion of space and spirit, intention and impact. This is where stories start. Where culture speaks. Where attention becomes emotion.
